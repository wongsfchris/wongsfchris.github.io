So it is only natural that I got myself studying accounting.

I also grew up being very fascinated with computers and the things I can do with it. I built a webpage using geocities (oh-oh, age-reveal) for the girl guides at my school. I learnt about IF THEN ELSE only in college, and it blew my mind to know that's how machines work. Programming.

Career

Career progression led me to be a project manager, and several years later, got my PMP certification too.

After being too comfortable in the SAP space, I decided to venture out to do data science. So here I am now :D
